# 🏨 **Travel, Tourism & Hospitality — Customer Retention & Dynamic Pricing Analysis**

Internship Project | Data Science & Analytics
Exploratory Data Analysis · Customer Segmentation · Churn Prediction · Pricing Dashboard

## 🏢 Business Problem
High cancellation rates lead to unsold inventory and revenue loss
Static pricing fails to capture demand fluctuations
Lack of customer insights limits targeted retention strategies
Inefficient decision-making due to absence of predictive analytics

## 🎯 Objectives
Reduce cancellation rates by identifying key factors influencing booking cancellations (e.g., lead time, deposit type, customer segment)
Optimize pricing strategies (ADR) by analyzing demand patterns and seasonal trends
Improve RevPAR by aligning pricing with periods of high and low demand
Enable targeted customer retention strategies through segmentation and behavioral analysis

## 🔍 Key Work
Data cleaning & feature engineering
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Customer segmentation
Cancellation prediction (Logistic Regression / Decision Tree)
Interactive dashboard (Tableau/Power BI)


## 🛠️ Tech Stack

Python (Pandas, NumPy), Seaborn, Matplotlib, Scikit-learn, Tableau/Power BI
